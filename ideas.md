## Идея 1: Османски пергамент / Ottoman Parchment

**Design Movement:** Историческа илюстрация + ръкописен стил (19-ти век)

**Core Principles:**
- Aged paper textures and ink-stained surfaces everywhere
- Handwritten-style typography mixed with serif display fonts
- Warm sepia/amber tones with deep forest greens and blood reds
- Every UI element feels like it was stamped, wax-sealed, or hand-drawn

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: deep parchment tan (oklch 0.82 0.06 80)
- Primary: dark forest green (oklch 0.35 0.12 150) — the color of the mountain forest
- Accent: blood red (oklch 0.45 0.22 25) — revolution and sacrifice
- Text: near-black ink (oklch 0.18 0.03 60)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric newspaper-column layout for the home screen
- Cards arranged like scattered documents on a wooden table
- Game board feels like a hand-drawn tactical map

**Signature Elements:**
- Worn paper texture overlay on all backgrounds
- Red wax seal motifs for buttons and badges
- Crossed pistols / sword decorative dividers

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards flip with a paper-fold animation
- Buttons feel like pressing a rubber stamp
- Transitions: slow, deliberate, like turning pages

**Animation:**
- Card reveal: fold-unfold with slight crinkle
- Page transitions: fade through sepia
- Hover: slight paper lift with shadow

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Playfair Display" – ornate serif for headings
- Body: "IM Fell English" – old-style serif for text
- UI labels: "Cinzel" – classical Roman caps for stats/labels

## Идея 2: Балканска нощ / Balkan Night

**Design Movement:** Dark fantasy + woodcut illustration aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Deep night-sky backgrounds with moonlit mountain silhouettes
- High contrast between dark backgrounds and warm golden/amber highlights
- Rough woodcut-style borders and decorative elements
- Dramatic lighting — cards and panels glow against darkness

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black with blue-green tint (oklch 0.12 0.04 220)
- Primary: warm gold (oklch 0.72 0.14 80) — firelight and glory
- Accent: deep crimson (oklch 0.42 0.20 20) — blood and revolution
- Surface: dark slate (oklch 0.20 0.03 240)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed dark hero with floating card panels
- Game board: dark felt table with glowing card slots
- Stats displayed as glowing ember counters

**Signature Elements:**
- Mountain silhouette skyline as decorative header
- Woodcut-style borders on all cards and panels
- Glowing ember/fire particle effects on key actions

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards glow when hovered, like embers fanned to life
- Actions feel weighty and consequential
- Transitions: dramatic cross-fades

**Animation:**
- Card draw: slide up from darkness with glow
- Stat increase: pulsing golden flash
- Turn transition: screen dims, new player name fades in

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Cinzel Decorative" – dramatic Roman caps
- Body: "Crimson Text" – elegant dark-mode serif
- Labels: "Cinzel" – consistent classical feel

## Идея 3: Хайдушка чета / Haydut Band (CHOSEN)

**Design Movement:** Illustrated historical board game — warm earthy tones, illustrated card art, tactical map aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Rich warm earth tones: ochre, burnt sienna, deep forest green, aged ivory
- Every surface has subtle linen/canvas texture — feels like a physical board game
- Cards look like real illustrated game cards with colored borders per type
- Game board feels like a tactical operations table with wooden elements

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: deep warm charcoal (oklch 0.18 0.025 55) — like aged walnut wood
- Surface: warm ivory/parchment (oklch 0.88 0.04 80) — for cards and panels
- Primary: deep forest green (oklch 0.38 0.10 148) — Хайдути color
- Accent: burnt sienna/copper (oklch 0.58 0.14 42) — warmth and action
- Danger: deep crimson (oklch 0.44 0.18 22) — Заптие/threat
- Gold: warm amber (oklch 0.72 0.12 78) — Чета points and glory
- Card colors: green, blue, red, yellow — vivid but slightly desaturated

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Home: full-bleed illustrated hero with side panel for navigation
- Card gallery: masonry-style grid with category filters
- Game setup: centered form on textured background
- Game board: split-screen — field (center) + player area (bottom) + stats (side)

**Signature Elements:**
- Linen canvas texture on all backgrounds
- Wooden frame/border aesthetic for panels and cards
- Crossed pistols decorative motif as dividers and icons

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards have satisfying flip animations when revealed
- Dragging/clicking cards feels physical and tactile
- Turn handoff: dramatic "pass device" screen with player name

**Animation:**
- Card flip: 3D Y-axis rotation (0.4s ease)
- Card hover: subtle lift with drop shadow
- Stat change: number counter animation with color flash
- Turn transition: full-screen overlay with player name

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Cinzel" – classical Roman caps for titles and headings
- Body: "Lora" – warm humanist serif for rules and descriptions
- UI: "Source Sans 3" – clean, readable for game stats and labels
</text>
